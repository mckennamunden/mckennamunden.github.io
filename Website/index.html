MCKENNA MUNDEN

McKenna is a 19 year old college student currently attending the University of Montana in Missoula, MT. She is pursuing a degree in digital marketing. She began photography at a young age but didn't start portrait photography until her freshman year of high school. She has photographed numerous senior portraits, families, a few weddings, and more. McKenna now works as a freelance photographer based in Missoula and hopes to further her photography career in the fashion industry along with continuing to perform music.
